Our Team

My name is Akshobhya, and I am delighted to share a glimpse of my journey with you. My academic journey commenced at the renowned Indian Institute of Technology Ropar, where I pursued my undergraduate studies. The rigorous academic environment and exposure to diverse subjects broadened my horizons and nurtured my passion for computer science.

For the past two years, I have been actively contributing my skills and knowledge as a part of the esteemed Samsung Research Institute in Bangalore. It has been an enriching experience, and I have had the opportunity to work on cutting-edge projects in the field of technology.

During my time at Samsung, a concerning incident occurred when one of my colleagues fell victim to a scam by unknowingly entering confidential details on a fake website. Witnessing the impact it had on him inspired me to take action. Driven by a sense of responsibility and concern for others' security, I launched a website called officialdomains.in. The platform aims to raise awareness and provide a secure space for users to verify the authenticity of websites before sharing sensitive information.

Thank you for taking the time to learn a little about me. I am eager to embrace the opportunities that lie ahead and contribute positively to the world of technology.
